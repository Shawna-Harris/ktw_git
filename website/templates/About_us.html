About Us

At Keys To Win we believe in the growth of the human spirit, soul, mind and condition. We understand that through education, diligence, compassion, and community we can help to eradicate barriers that contribute to poverty and crime. Our hope is to instill a love of learning how to code and how to create in our participants. Thus, leading to equitable, safe, and diverse communities.
